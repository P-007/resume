Day care centre for the children with mentally impaired.

Health is the wealth of any society but it is possible only when every individual determines to live healthy and have access to health care systems. It is true that India raises the maximum number of doctors every year compared to any other country but it is also a painful truth that no one is interested to serve the poor and needy who live in disease prone forest and remote villages. Very few Rural NGO services been in India then thousands of people, particularly children would have passed away due to simple diseases. It is well a known fact that the cost of few tablets is just Rs.5 but the travel expense to buy the tablets costs more than Rs.100. Such is the medical facility existing even today in majority of tribal and remote villages. 

Realizing the health care need of the target villages and target communities particularly the rural and Tribal poor people.

a) Health care mobile clinics in remote Urban and rural area.

 

b) Health awareness education through visual and folk drama medium.

 

c)Village Health workers training.

 

d) HIV/AIDS awareness and provide medicine.

e) Coverage on pre-neo and post natal care.

 

f) Health linkages with Government and NGO medical institution and schools.

g) Associating with government and NGO medical institution on national health care programs.
